THE MANDATE AND COMMISSION

By the grace of God, Apostle Mark Abogonye Paul gave his life to Jesus Christ in the year 1998 August in living faith church Otukpo, Benue state. Then he began his Christian faith, as a new convert, he was passionate about souls and burning for kingdom assignment.
In the year 2001, I was on my way to Port Harcourt in Rivers state to look for job. While waiting on the Lord in prayer and fasting. We reached Okigwe in Imo State, the vehicle stop for passenger to eat and refreshed themselves, I couldn’t come down from the vehicle, while I was praying suddenly my eyes open and I saw a revelation a “woman back a child on her back with a ragged, hacking and a man carried a bag of beans about 50kg on his shoulder. And God spoke to me, saying Mark this is not how a created these people to be , you are looking for job but I have given you a job. Them, I saw on a screen in the front of the vehicle a scripture Isaiah 49:8
”thus saith the lord, in an acceptable time I have heard thee, and in a day of salvation have I helped thee: and will preserve thee, and give thee for the covenant of the people, to establish the earth, to cause to inherit the desolate heritage”
